Biography of Sundar Pichai

Early Life

Sundar Pichai was born in Madurai, India, in 1972. He earned his degree from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur in Metallurgical Engineering.

Sundar Pichai during his early years.

Technology is an enabler for greater equality. - Sundar Pichai

Career

Sundar Pichai joined Google in 2004, where he led the product management and innovation efforts for a suite of Google's client software products.

Sundar Pichai at a Google event.

A lot of our competition is with ourselves. We tend to stay focused. - Sundar Pichai

CEO OF GOOGLE

In 2015, Sundar Pichai was appointed CEO of Google. Under his leadership, Google has continued to grow and innovate.

Personal Life

Sundar Pichai lives in the San Francisco Bay Area with his family. He is known for his humility and dedication to his work.
